1. Scope

This policy applies to physical desktop PC purchases made from [Business Name] for delivery within the United Kingdom.

2. 14-day cooling-off period

Under the Consumer Contracts Regulations, you have the right to cancel your order within 14 days from the day after you receive your goods.

To cancel, email [Contact Email] with your order details. After cancelling, you must return the goods within 14 days.

3. Condition of returned goods

Please return goods in a condition that reflects reasonable handling. You may inspect the goods as you would in a shop, but you may be responsible for any reduction in value caused by handling beyond this.

4. Return shipping costs

For cancellations under the cooling-off period, you are responsible for return shipping costs unless we state otherwise.

If goods are faulty, not as described, or not fit for purpose, we will cover reasonable return costs.

5. Faulty goods and your legal rights

Your rights under the Consumer Rights Act 2015 are not affected by this policy.

If your goods are faulty, not as described, or not fit for purpose, contact us at [Contact Email]. Depending on the timing and circumstances, you may be entitled to repair, replacement, price reduction, or refund, including a short-term right to reject within 30 days where applicable.

6. Refund method and timing

Refunds are processed back through Stripe to your original payment method.

For cancelled orders, we will issue your refund within 14 days of receiving the returned goods, or within 14 days of receiving evidence that you have sent them back, whichever is earlier.

For faulty goods, refunds are made after assessment and confirmation of your entitlement under consumer law.

7. How to start a return or refund request

Email [Contact Email] and include your name, order details, and the reason for return.
