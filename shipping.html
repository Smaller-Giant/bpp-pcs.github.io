Shipping Information

We ship physical desktop PCs to addresses in the United Kingdom only.

Delivery Area

Orders are delivered within England, Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland. We do not currently offer international delivery.

Estimated Delivery Timeframe

  • Standard desktop orders: usually 2 to 5 working days after dispatch.
  • Higher-spec or custom-assembly desktops may require additional preparation time.

Shipping Cost

Shipping is charged at a flat rate of GBP 24.99 per desktop order.

Orders with a total value over GBP 2,500 qualify for free UK shipping.

Order Processing

Orders are processed Monday to Friday, excluding UK public holidays. Paid orders are normally prepared within 1 to 2 working days before dispatch.

Tracking details will be emailed once dispatched.
