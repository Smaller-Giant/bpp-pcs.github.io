Shipping Information

We ship physical desktop PCs to addresses in the United Kingdom only.

Delivery Area

Orders are delivered within England, Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland. We do not currently offer international delivery.

Build Time

All systems are built and tested before shipping. Estimated build time is 2 to 4 business days and does not include delivery time.

Delivery and Tracking

PCs are shipped using UK courier services with tracking. Tracking details are emailed once dispatched.

Shipping Cost

Shipping costs (if applicable) are confirmed at checkout.

Order Processing

Orders are processed Monday to Friday, excluding UK public holidays. Dispatch occurs once build and testing are complete.

Shipping Support

For shipping issues, email support@bpppcs.com. For warranty returns, BPP PCs will arrange courier collection.
