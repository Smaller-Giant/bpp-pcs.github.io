1. Scope

This policy applies to physical desktop PCs sold by BPP PCs for delivery within the United Kingdom.

2. Returns Window

Customers may return products within 21 days of delivery.

Please contact support@bpppcs.com before returning any product so we can approve the return and provide instructions.

3. Return Approval and Condition

Returns must be approved before items are sent back. Returned goods should be in suitable condition and packaged safely for transport.

4. Return Costs

For change-of-mind returns, the customer may be responsible for return shipping. If the product is faulty, BPP PCs will arrange courier collection.

5. Faulty Goods

Your rights under the Consumer Rights Act 2015 are not affected. Remedies may include repair, replacement, price reduction, or refund as provided by law.

6. Refunds

Approved refunds are sent through Stripe to the original payment method.

7. Contact

To start a return, contact support@bpppcs.com and include your name, order details, and reason for return. We will confirm approval and next steps.
